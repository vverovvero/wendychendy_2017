WENDY CHEN

ABOUT ME

I am a developer and artist, seeking creative challenges.

Hi, my name is Wendy. I am expecting to graduate from Yale University with a B.S. in Computer Science in May 2017. I have worked as an Art & Production Intern at Walt Disney Animation Studios and as a Production Engineering Intern at Blue Sky Studios.

My past projects include a CG animated short film, a VR video game, and a studio production tool. I learn quickly and embrace challenges.

I am equally happy working on the front end or back end. I can adapt between roles as an artist, programmer, team player, and team leader.

Because I have both a technical understanding and an artistic eye, I am ready to create something we've never seen before.
